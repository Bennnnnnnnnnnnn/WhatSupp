Your Recommended Supplements

Tier 1: Essential

Creatine Monohydrate

Strong Evidence
Benefits:
  • Increases muscle power and strength
  • Enhances muscle growth
  • Improves high-intensity exercise performance
  • May support brain health
Dosage & Timing:

3-5g daily - Can be taken anytime, with or without food. No loading phase needed.

Research:

600+ studies support creatine's effectiveness. Meta-analyses show 5-15% strength gains and enhanced muscle growth.

Whey Protein

Strong Evidence
Benefits:
  • Supports muscle protein synthesis
  • Aids in muscle recovery
  • Convenient protein source
  • Rich in essential amino acids
Dosage & Timing:

20-40g post-workout or as needed to meet daily protein goals (0.8-1g per lb bodyweight).

Research:

Extensive research shows whey protein optimizes muscle protein synthesis and recovery when combined with resistance training.

Tier 2: Beneficial

Caffeine

Strong Evidence
Benefits:
  • Increases energy and alertness
  • Enhances exercise performance
  • Improves focus and concentration
  • May boost metabolic rate
Dosage & Timing:

100-400mg 30-45 minutes before workout. Avoid within 6 hours of bedtime.

Research:

Meta-analyses show 3-7% performance improvements in strength and endurance activities. Well-tolerated by most individuals.

Vitamin D3

Moderate Evidence
Benefits:
  • Supports bone health
  • May enhance muscle function
  • Supports immune system
  • Important for overall health
Dosage & Timing:

1000-4000 IU daily with fat-containing meal for better absorption. Get blood levels tested.

Research:

Many people are deficient. Studies show benefits for bone health and potential muscle function improvements in deficient individuals.

Tier 3: Consider If Budget Allows

Beta-Alanine

Moderate Evidence
Benefits:
  • Reduces muscle fatigue
  • Improves muscular endurance
  • Enhances performance in 1-4 minute activities
  • May reduce muscle soreness
Dosage & Timing:

3-5g daily split into smaller doses (0.8-1g) to avoid tingling sensation. Take with meals.

Research:

Studies show 2-3% performance improvements in activities lasting 1-4 minutes. Effects more pronounced in trained individuals.

Fish Oil (Omega-3)

Moderate Evidence
Benefits:
  • Supports heart health
  • May reduce inflammation
  • Supports joint health
  • Important for overall wellness
Dosage & Timing:

1-3g combined EPA/DHA daily with meals to improve absorption and reduce fishy aftertaste.

Research:

Strong evidence for cardiovascular health. Mixed results for exercise performance, but important for overall health.
